Project Overview

Create a highly polished, interactive, mobile-first web experience called:

"Which Electric Scooter Is Right For Me?"

This is a pilot UX/UI concept for e-trotineti.rs, a future e-mobility hub that helps users discover whether they are better suited for Dualtron, Teverun, or Kaabo.

The goal is NOT to create an ecommerce store.

The goal is to:

educate users,
guide them through a decision-making process,
help them understand differences between brands,
recommend the most suitable brand and scooter category,
provide a professional expert-style conclusion.

The experience should feel like a premium digital product, not a traditional website.
